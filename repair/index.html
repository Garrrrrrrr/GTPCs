Services

  • PC troubleshooting for boot issues, crashes, poor performance, and hardware problems.
  • GPU, CPU, motherboard, RAM, storage, PSU, cooling, and case installation.
  • Gaming PC upgrades and compatibility checks.
  • Windows setup, driver updates, BIOS updates, and basic software cleanup.
  • Thermal paste replacement, cooling checks, fan setup, and airflow improvements.
  • Custom build support and rebuilds using new or existing components.

How It Works

Email GTPCS with your PC specs, the issue you are seeing, any recent changes, and whether you already have replacement parts. I will review the details and confirm the next step before any work is started.

Repair work is handled manually. Some issues require diagnostics before a reliable estimate or recommendation can be given.

Local Edmonton Service

GTPCS is run locally in Edmonton. Pickup, drop-off, or meeting details are confirmed directly before service. Do not bring or ship a system until the repair request has been reviewed and accepted.

Important Notes

  • Repair outcomes cannot be guaranteed on failing, damaged, liquid-damaged, or intermittent hardware.
  • Back up important data before service whenever possible.
  • Parts, replacement components, shipping, and special-order items are quoted separately when needed.
  • High-value systems and components may require additional review before service is accepted.
